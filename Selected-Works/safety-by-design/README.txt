IMAGES FOR THIS CASE STUDY
==========================
Drop your images here using these exact names. Any you leave out
are simply skipped on the page (a half-filled project still looks tidy).
The build script (node build/generate.js) finds them automatically.

  cover.jpg       big image at the top of the case study (16:9 looks best)
  sketch.jpg      Process step 1
  cad.jpg         Process step 2
  prototype.jpg   Process step 3
  final.jpg       Process step 4
  decisions.jpg   image beside the "Key decisions" text (4:3)
  outcome.jpg     image in the "Outcome" section (16:9)
  gallery-1.jpg   optional extra images — gallery-2.jpg, gallery-3.jpg, ...

Tips
  - Lowercase, hyphenated names. The live server is case-sensitive.
  - ~1600px wide, JPG at ~80% quality. Huge files make the page slow.
  - .jpg / .jpeg / .png / .webp all work.
